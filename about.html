Since 1986

Decades of compounding capability.

Samona Industries Inc. was founded by Samuel and Ramona Rymer and exists to build, manage, and preserve a professionally structured family enterprise focused on long-term growth,strategic oversight, and generational continuity.

Through disciplined governance, responsible stewardship, and a commitment to enduring family values, the Company seeks to create and sustain opportunities and assets for current and future generations.


We are committed to:

  • — Preserving and growing family owned assets.
  • — Supporting sustainable long-term growth.
  • — Encouraging disciplined governance and accountability.
  • — Build a system that supports future generations.
