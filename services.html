What we do

Six practices, one accountable team.

Each engagement is led by a senior partner, scoped against measurable success criteria, and delivered with the same craft standard we’ve held since 1986.

01

Precision Manufacturing

Tolerance-critical machining, fabrication, and assembly for aerospace, energy, and defense customers. ITAR-registered facilities.

02

Energy & Power Systems

Design, retrofit, and lifecycle support for power generation, transmission, and renewables. Grid-scale to distributed assets.

03

Infrastructure Delivery

EPC and program management for industrial facilities, ports, water, and transport corridors. Greenfield and brownfield.

04

Industrial Automation

Process control, robotics integration, and digital twin services that move the OEE needle — typically by 12-18 points.

05

Asset Optimization

Reliability engineering, predictive maintenance, and turnaround planning. We extend asset life by an average of 23%.

06

Compliance & Safety

ISO, OSHA, and IEC program design, audit, and remediation across your global operations. Audit-ready in 90 days.

Our process

Four phases. No surprises.

Every engagement runs through the same disciplined framework — refined across 420 projects.

01

Discover

On-site walkdowns, stakeholder interviews, and data review to map the real constraints, not the documented ones.

02

Design

Engineering deliverables with explicit acceptance criteria, signed off by ops, safety, and finance before fabrication.

03

Deliver

Build, install, and commission with our own field crews — not subcontractors carrying our badge.

04

Sustain

12-month performance warranty plus optional reliability services. We measure success at month 13, not at handover.
